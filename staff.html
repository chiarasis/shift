OUR STAFF

Led by Director Zenani Mandela-Dlamini, our multidisciplinary team integrates temporal sciences, digital preservation, and outreach. The Timeline Analysis Department studies interdimensional patterns, Historical Preservation one archives decoherence events, and the Public Outreach one shares insights through interactive experiences, making multiverse research accessible.
