ABOUT THE INSITUTE

FOUNDATION AND MISSION

Founded in 1940, SHIFT (Scientific Historical Institute for Timelines) was established to address the known phenomenon of timeline decoherence. While the concept was already studied, SHIFT focuses on brand logos, as their changes are subtle yet verifiable by anyone. By cataloguing and analysing these shifts, known as the Mandela Effect, logos serve as stable reference points to reveal and understand interdimensional interactions.

FUNDS

SHIFT is funded as a public institution, with governmental support aimed at fostering unbiased, collaborative research across timelines. This funding is complemented by grants and donations from scientific organizations interested in expanding the understanding of multiverse theory and its applications.

DEPARTMENTS

TIMELINE ANALYSIS

It studies and compares data across realities, analyzing timeline shifts to identify decoherence patterns. This advances understanding of multiverse interactions and their measurable impact on brand identities.

HISTORICAL PRESERVATION

It is dedicated to archiving and cataloguing shifts in brand iconography to maintain a comprehensive record of timeline variations. It ensures the integrity and accessibility of data for future analysis and research.

PUBLIC OUTREACH

Engaging directly with the public, it shares the Institute findings and promotes interactive experiences: at our Delta headquarters, visitors can explore decoherences with a machine that compares brand logos across realities.

THE ARCHIVE

SHIFT conducts its research through direct observation and data collection from the five currently known timelines: Sigma, Xi, Theta, Delta, Lambda.
