WELCOME TO SHIFT

SHIFT (Scientific Historical Institute for Timelines) operates within the multiverse, studying timeline decoherences and its impact on brand identity. By analysing and archiving subtle shifts in brand identity, we aim to uncover patterns and deepen the understanding of these interdimensional anomalies.

DISCOVERING THE MULTIVERSE

THE LINEAR ILLUSION

We once believed in a single, unchanging reality, but SHIFT revealed our universe coexists with four interconnected parallels, each influencing the others. This discovery reshaped our understanding of space, time, and existence.

THE PHENOMENON OF DECOHERENCE

Decoherence happens when energy events weaken barriers between universes, causing fragments to intermingle. Subtle changes in logos, like color or shape, reveal these interactions and their connections.

TRACES AND RESEARCH

When decoherence ends, universes separate, leaving small anomalies as traces of their interaction. These inconsistencies offer insights into parallel realities, helping us understand the shift and their connections.
