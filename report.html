REPORT DECOHERENCE

If you’ve noticed an anomaly in reality or believe you’ve experienced a Mandela Effect, complete this form to help us collect valuable data for research and verification purposes. Reports are submitted under your name; however, SHIFT ensures complete confidentiality regarding your identity. Once submitted, a report cannot be modified or deleted.

WHAT TO REPORT

You can report any inconsistencies in logos, graphics, or brand elements. Provide detailed descriptions of anomalies, specifying differences from the expected version. Attach photos, documents, or links if available to help our team verify and analyze the effects accurately.
